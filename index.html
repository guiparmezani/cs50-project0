A bit about myself

In early 2015, I got hired to work for a tech company in Austin, TX, and that changed my life. My contract was for two years. I'm also very passionate about computers and how they work. Taking this course is something that I'll enjoy doing, and it'll also add up to my skill set and knowledge base. I'm excited!
